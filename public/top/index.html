Carsharing

  • You will experience a simulation of how Tom, an automobile owner and driver, can use a mobile app to rent a car provided by an automotive company.
  • This new type of service will help the automotive company attract and retain customers. The design of future cars and services can be based on this new sources of customer and vehicle data.
  • You will use a mobile app on an iOS phone and experience how Tom can find an available car located near him and reserve the car. You will experience how Tom can review his driving behavior, which is analyzed by Driver Behavior service on Bluemix.

Driving Behavior Analysis

  • You can use your iPhone as a sensor in the car to collect telemetry data in real-time and see the driver behavior results.
